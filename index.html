About Me

Bio

My name is Chris, but my friends call me Stizzy. I am a waxing twenty something who has recently dove into coding.

My journey into coding has been a long but gradual one. I began in sales operations and finance and analytics, where I learned the basics of working with data and data tools. From there, I leaned further into my technical skillsets and gained greater knowledge of SQL and extensive knowledge of Tableau, while at the same time working with our company's product and engineering team and becoming exposed to various technical concepts. My most recent role in consulting was a move away from day-to-day technical work, but it exposed me to a wider range of technical uses just outside of data analysis.

Combining that background with my desire to solve problems and develop solutions, I knew that coding was the right path for me.

About This Site

This site itself has simple concepts, but the layout, the colors, and the content were thoughtfully chosen to reflect a part of who I am.

The overall color scheme is a grayscale, as I have had my phone display in black and white for about four years now, and I have grown to appreciate the muted color scheme that is in contrast with the vibrant colors typically thrown at us. However, I chose to color each title as a shade of teal (my favorite color).

This site's layout began with an attempt to include as many features as I possibly could in the first section of the site. As one might expect for an early learner of anything, I became quickly overwhelmed trying to do too much too soon. I realized that, reflected upon what is most important now, and chose to go with a simpler layout to ensure that I showcase the right information prior to adding excess features.

I am sure that I will learn more about best practices in website presentation and find obvious faults in this method as I learn more. Yet, I felt it was important for my first portfolio to favor aspects of my style.

Thank you for taking the time to view my work!
